# Retail-Sale-Analytics-Dashboard
A Retail Performance Intelligence Dashboard built in Microsoft Excel using POS/ERP data to analyze sales, returns, and customer behavior, featuring KPI cards, pivot-based insights, trend analysis, and data-driven recommendations to optimize discount strategy, product mix, and profitability.

The project focuses on identifying sales trends, understanding product and category performance, and improving profitability through data-driven insights.

## ‚ùì Key Business Questions (KPIs)
The dashboard answers the following questions:
- Which states generate the highest and lowest total sales?
- How does sales vary across different sales channels?
- Which product categories contribute the most to revenue?
- Which items generate the highest sales and quantities?
- Which products have the highest return counts?
- Is there a relationship between high sales and high returns?
- How does category-wise revenue differ across states?

## üîç Key Insights (Summary)
- A small number of states contribute a disproportionately high share of total sales, indicating regional concentration.
- Certain product categories drive most of the revenue, while some high-quantity items show lower profitability due to discounts and returns.
- Sales channels differ significantly in performance, with online delivery showing higher return rates compared to in-store purchases.
- Higher sales volumes do not always correlate with higher profitability, highlighting the impact of discounts and returns.
